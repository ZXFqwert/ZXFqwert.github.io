Research Interests

My research focuses on the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Security. I am particularly interested in:

  • Robustness and Adversarial Attacks/Defenses in Machine Learning
  • Privacy-Preserving AI (e.g., Federated Learning, Differential Privacy)
  • Security and Fairness of Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Trustworthy and Explainable AI Systems

The goal is to build intelligent systems that are not only powerful but also secure, reliable, and aligned with human values.
